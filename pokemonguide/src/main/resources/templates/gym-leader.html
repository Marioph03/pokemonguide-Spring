Brock

Brock is a Rock-Type Pokémon Trainer and the Pewter City Gym Leader. He is known for his rock-solid defense and is the first Gym Leader that players face in the Pokémon games.

Misty

Misty is a Water-Type Pokémon Trainer and the Cerulean City Gym Leader. She is known for her strong bond with Water-Type Pokémon and her cheerful personality.
